Guitar to Alto Sax Transposer

I decided to program this to help me on the trasnposition of guitar notes to alto sax register key since I use apps like Songster to get some stuff.

Why?

The Alto Sax is tunned on Eb key and the guitar is tunned on the C register key, so, every note on guitar must be transposed 1 and a half tone down to sounds correct in the alto sax.

For example, the C note on the guitar, or even on the piano, sounds like an A on the alto sax because it's tunned 1 tone and a half up compared to the C note.

The Transposer

Shut the fuck up and give me my notes.

For that, wrtie below the notes to be transposed separated by comma and press entrer. Use N# and Nb for half tones.

Example: A#, Bb
