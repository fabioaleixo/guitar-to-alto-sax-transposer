Guitar to Alto Sax Transposer

I decided to program this to help me on the trasnposition of guitar notes to alto sax register key since I use apps like Songster to get some stuff.

Why?

The Alto Sax is tunned on Eb register key while the guitar is tunned on the C register key, so, every note on the alto sax must be transposed 1 and a half tone down to sounds like the guitar's C.

For example, the A note on the alto sax, sounds like an C on the guitar or piano.

The Transposer

Shut the fuck up and give me my notes.

For that, wrtie below the notes separated by comma as played on the guitar to be transposed and press entrer.

PS: Use N# and Nb for half tones. Example: A#, Bb
